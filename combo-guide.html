⚡ Combo Guide

Learn how to convert hits into massive damage.

1. Starting Strings

Pick fast, safe openers like:

  • 11 or 12 (fast jabs)
  • F3 or F4 (forward advancing mids)

2. Launchers

A launcher pops the enemy up for follow-ups.

Examples:

  • Sub-Zero: Back 1, 2
  • Scorpion: 2, 1, Spear

3. Jump-Ins

After launching, jump forward and press 2 or 3 for combo extension.

4. Enders

Finish your combo with:

  • Special Move
  • Hard Knockdown
  • Side Switch

5. Example Beginner Combo

1, 1 → Launcher → Jump 2 → Special Move

Practice until your execution becomes muscle memory.
