Kung Lao Combos

Hat spins, dive kicks, and fast combos for aggressive players.

Hat Spin Combo

Inputs: Back + 2, Forward + 3, 1

Spin your hat to start combo, follow up with fast attacks.

Dive Kick Combo

Inputs: Up + 4, 1, 2

Jump and dive kick opponent, chain into 3-hit combo.

Teleport Attack Combo

Inputs: Back + 3, Forward + 2, 4

Teleport behind opponent and continue combo.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Down, Back, High Kick (Close)

Signature finisher with Kung Lao’s hat.
