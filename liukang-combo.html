Liu Kang Combos

Learn Liu Kang’s fast striking combos in MK1 (2023)

Fireball Combo

Inputs: Back + 2, Forward + 1, 2

Launch a fireball and follow with a rapid 2-hit punch combo.

High Kick Starter

Inputs: Forward + 4, Down + 2, 1

Start with a high kick and juggle into a 3-hit combo.

Flying Dragon Combo

Inputs: Down + 3, Up + 4, 2

Launch the enemy and finish with aerial strikes.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Back, Forward, High Kick (Close)

Iconic finishing move to defeat your opponent.
