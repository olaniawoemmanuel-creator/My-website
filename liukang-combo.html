Liu Kang Combos

Fast kicks, fireballs, and acrobatic combos for all occasions.

Dragon Kick Combo

Inputs: Forward + 4, 2, 3

Start with Dragon Kick and chain a 3-hit combo.

Fireball Combo

Inputs: Back + 1, 2, 4

Launch a fireball, then follow up with quick punches.

Jump Kick Combo

Inputs: Up + 4, 1, 2

Jump onto opponent and deliver a fast kick combo.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Down, Back, High Kick (Close)

Finisher that unleashes Liu Kang’s signature dragon attack.
