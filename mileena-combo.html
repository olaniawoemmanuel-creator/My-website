Mileena Combos

Learn Mileena’s brutal combos in MK1 (2023)

Teleport Sai Strike

Inputs: Back + 3, Forward + 2, 1

Teleport behind the opponent and attack with sais.

Leg Sweep Combo

Inputs: Down + 4, Forward + 1, 2

Sweep the opponent’s legs and follow up with strikes.

Aerial Sai Combo

Inputs: Up + 4, Down + 2, 1

Jump attack with sais and chain into punches.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Back, Forward, High Punch (Close)

Finish enemies with Mileena’s signature fatality.
