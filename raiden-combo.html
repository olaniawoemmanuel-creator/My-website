Raiden Combos

Electrify your matches with Raiden combos in MK1 (2023)

Lightning Strike

Inputs: Down + 2, Forward + 1, 2

Stun the opponent with lightning then combo punch.

Teleport Punch

Inputs: Back + 3, Up + 2, 1

Teleport behind your opponent and hit with a 2-hit combo.

Thunder Juggle

Inputs: Down + 1, Up + 3, 2, 4

Launch opponent into air and juggle with electric strikes.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Back, Forward, Low Punch (Close)

Electrify and finish off your enemy.
