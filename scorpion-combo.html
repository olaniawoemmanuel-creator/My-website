Scorpion Combos

Master Scorpion’s iconic moves and fatalities in MK1 (2023)

Spear Combo

Inputs: Back + 2, Forward + 4, 1, 2

Classic teleport spear starter followed by a fast punch combo.

Teleport Punch Combo

Inputs: Back + 3, Forward + 1, 2, 3

Teleport behind opponent and follow up with a 3-hit combo.

Flaming Uppercut Combo

Inputs: Down + 2, Up + 1, 2

Launches the enemy into the air, perfect for juggling.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Forward, Down, High Punch (Close)

Finisher that teleports Scorpion and delivers his iconic attack.
