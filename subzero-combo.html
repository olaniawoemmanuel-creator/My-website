Sub-Zero Combos

Master Sub-Zero’s freezing and combo techniques in MK1 (2023)

Ice Ball Starter

Inputs: Forward + 2, 1, 2

Freeze your opponent and follow up with a quick punch combo.

Slide Combo

Inputs: Down + 4, Forward + 3, 2

Slide under enemy and hit with a 3-hit combo.

Frozen Uppercut

Inputs: Down + 2, Up + 1, 2

Launches the opponent into the air for juggling combos.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Back, Forward, Low Punch (Close)

Freeze the enemy and finish with Sub-Zero’s iconic fatality.
