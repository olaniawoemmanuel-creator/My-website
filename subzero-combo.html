Sub-Zero Combos

Freeze your opponents and chain deadly icy combos.

Freeze Starter Combo

Inputs: Forward + 2, Back + 1, 2

Start with freezing attack, then follow up with a 3-hit combo.

Slide Combo

Inputs: Down + 4, 2, 3

Slide into opponent and chain a quick uppercut combo.

Ice Ball Combo

Inputs: Back, Forward + 1, 2, 4

Throw ice ball to freeze opponent and follow up with fast punches.

Classic Fatality

Inputs: Forward, Down, Forward, High Punch (Close)

Finisher that freezes and finishes the opponent in style.
